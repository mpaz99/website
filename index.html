About

Hello, my name is Mauricio Paz and I am a Digital Media creator. I am currently a Junior at Molloy College and this website is a culmination of my work. This portfolio represents my Video Projects and photography throughout my years at school and as a content creator. My skill set combines creative, technical, and business-related experience to develop custom social media content. I have a passion for applying my creative skills in digital contexts with clear and observable outcomes. Combining creative work with marketing experience is my ultimate career goal. I am proficient in adobe premiere,photoshop, indesign and lightroom. I also know some basic after effects and am determined to learn and familiarize myself more with the platform. I started out with the passion for photography, but as I progressed in my years in college I began to identify my abilities in video production and editing. I determined myself to be proficient with this new media to expand my creative knowledge.
